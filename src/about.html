How we got started

One day in our mom’s garage…just kidding. Dewey Design Labs got started by two technology enthusiasts. One a UX Designer, the other a Developer/Creative Technologist. We had the perfect mix to make the perfect things.

Why do we do it

We realize the future is digital and we want our clients to be at the forefront of it. We do what we love but for people that love what they do. This allows you to be the best in what you do.

How we do it

We use user-centered process that develops solutions users will actually use consistently. Our process allows us to customize solutions specifically for clients and not just provide a cookie cutter solution.

Where do we do it

The two founders, Darsh and Ranjay, are Washington, DC area natives. We live just outside of the city but work in the city.
